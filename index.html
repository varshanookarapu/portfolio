ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Varsha.

I’m a data analyst with a background in quality analysis and a passion for turning raw data into clear, meaningful insights. I currently work as a Quality Analyst at Stride Systems and hold a Master’s degree in Computational Sciences from Laurentian University.

My journey into data analytics began during a business analytics course in my master’s program, where I discovered how data visualization and storytelling can support better decision-making. I enjoy the entire analytics process — from cleaning and transforming data to building dashboards that uncover patterns and help solve real-world problems.

I primarily work with Excel, SQL, and Power BI, and have experience with Python and R from academic projects. Recently, I’ve been focusing on building a strong portfolio that highlights Excel-based data cleaning, SQL exploratory analysis, and Power BI dashboards using DAX. I’m a curious, adaptable, and reliable team player who takes pride in delivering thorough and well-structured work.

I’m seeking entry-level to junior data analyst opportunities in flexible startup or corporate environments that value collaboration and continuous learning. When I’m not working with data, you’ll usually find me reading a good book or exploring new hiking trails.
